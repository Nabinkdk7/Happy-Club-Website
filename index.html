Guidelines

Happy Club Rules

Every member is expected to follow these rules.

1

Registration Required

Every new member must register by providing their name, a short introduction, signature, and thumbprint.

2

Active Participation

Every member should actively participate and maintain regular attendance at club gatherings.

3

Timely Contributions

Each member must pay their assigned contribution on time. Repeated excuses are not acceptable by the club.

4

Late Payment Fine

If a member fails to pay on their assigned turn, they must pay an additional fine equal to two days of tea expenses.

📌

Important Note

Each member's payment turn comes twice every month. Paying on your assigned turn is mandatory. Regular on-time payments keep the club running smoothly for everyone.
